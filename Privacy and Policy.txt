Privacy Policy for Blood Hub
Effective Date: 06/07/2026

1. Introduction
Welcome to Blood Hub. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our mobile application Blood Hub.

By using Blood Hub, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect
2.1 Information You Provide
Account Information: When you register, we may collect your name, email address, phone number, and blood type.

Location Information: Your current location to help find nearby blood donors or donation centers.

Health Information: Blood type, donation history, and medical eligibility information (with your explicit consent).

User Content: Blood requests, donor responses, and any messages you send through the App.

2.2 Information Automatically Collected
Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers.

Usage Data: How you interact with the App, features you use, and time spent.

Log Data: IP address, app crash reports, and access times.

3. How We Use Your Information
We use your information to:

Connect blood donors with recipients in need

Send urgent blood request notifications

Verify user identities to prevent misuse

Improve and optimize App performance

Comply with legal and regulatory requirements

Respond to emergencies and critical health situations

4. Permissions We Use
Blood Hub requires the following permissions:

Permission	Purpose
INTERNET	To connect to our servers and match donors with recipients
CAMERA	To scan medical documents or blood donation certificates (optional)
READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE	To upload profile pictures or medical documents
CALL_PHONE	To directly call emergency contacts or blood donors (only with your action)
5. How We Share Your Information
We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information:

With Other Users: Your blood type and general location (not exact address) will be visible to potential donors/recipients.

With Healthcare Providers: If you request, we may share information with blood banks or hospitals.

Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect safety during emergencies.

Service Providers: Third parties who help us operate the App (hosting, analytics, etc.).

6. Data Security
We implement industry-standard security measures including:

Encryption of sensitive data

Secure server infrastructure

Regular security audits

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. Data Retention
We retain your information:

As long as your account is active

To comply with legal obligations

For legitimate business purposes

You may request deletion of your data by contacting us.

8. Children's Privacy
Blood Hub is not intended for users under 13 years of age (or under 17 in some regions for medical apps). We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Your Rights
Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

Access your personal data

Correct inaccurate data

Delete your data

Withdraw consent at any time

Opt out of communications
