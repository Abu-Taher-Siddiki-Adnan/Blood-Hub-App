Why it matters

A life-or-death problem
needs a real solution

The old way is broken

Families post frantically on Facebook. They call every contact. They wait. Minutes turn to hours. Every minute without blood is critical — and the system wasn't built to help them.

Blood Hub changes that

Create a request in seconds. Compatible donors in your district get notified instantly via push notification. They respond, confirm, and you get their contact. One platform. Zero wasted time.

Core features

Everything you need
to save a life

Blood Hub is a complete platform for blood donation management — designed for real emergencies across Bangladesh.

01
Verified accounts

Secure registration with email verification. Only real, confirmed accounts can donate or post requests.

02
Blood requests

Create a detailed request and instantly alert compatible donors in your district. Takes under 30 seconds.

03
Donor search

Search by blood group, district, and upazila. Filter by availability. Find the right person, fast.

04
Instant notifications

Firebase Cloud Messaging delivers emergency alerts in real time. Donors are notified the moment a match is found.

05
Donation tracking

Track your donation history, eligibility window, and lifetime contributions — all from your profile.

06
Profile control

Manage your availability, blood group, location, and preferences. Go offline when unavailable, back on in seconds.

FAQ

Common questions

Yes — Blood Hub is completely free. There are no ads, no subscription fees, and no hidden costs. It's a community platform built for public good.

You need a verified account to post requests and contact donors. This is to protect donors from spam and ensure all interactions are between real, verified users.

Your data is used only for matching you with blood requests or donors in your area. Contact information is shared only when a donor explicitly accepts a request. We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

An internet connection is required to post requests, receive notifications, and search for donors in real time. Basic profile information is cached locally for offline viewing.

Blood Hub currently supports Android 5.0 and above. iOS support is being planned. You can reach out via email to be notified when it launches.

From your profile page, you can toggle your availability on or off at any time. When you're marked unavailable, you won't receive donation request notifications.
